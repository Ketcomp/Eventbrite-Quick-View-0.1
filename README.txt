II 		Setup:

	1. Type "chrome://extensions" in the address-bar of Google Chrome OR go to the extensions page through the settings.
	2. Click the check-box that reads "Developer Options"
	3. Now click on "Load Unpacked Extensions" and using the file browser, browse to the folder "Eventbrite Quick-Look Extension" and click OK
	4. Check the check-box under the extension's tab that says "Enable"
	5. Your extension icon should now appear on the top-right corner of the address-bar.

III		Usage:

	1. Make sure you are connected to the internet and click on the icon of the 'Eventbrite Quick-View' extension.
	2. The most popular four events near your current location should appear.
	3. The extension also shows top 4 events happening on the next weekend - albeit spanning a much larger area around your current location.
